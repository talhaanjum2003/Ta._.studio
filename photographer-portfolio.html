About Me

I Tell Stories
Through Light

Born and raised in the heart of Islamabad, I've spent over a decade capturing the soul of Pakistan — from the golden spires of the Faisal Mosque at dawn to the snow-dusted peaks of the Karakoram. My lens is my voice, and every frame is a sentence in a story worth telling.

I combine technical mastery with an emotional sensitivity that turns ordinary moments into extraordinary images. Whether it's a whispered laugh at a wedding, the last light catching a mountain ridge, or the quiet dignity of a street vendor — I see the extraordinary in the everyday.
